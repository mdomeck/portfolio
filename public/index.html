About Me

I am a software developer from Columbus, OH. In my past experience I was working in a remote position supporting corporate childcare programs across the country. I managed substitute teachers and was responsible for interviewing, hiring and training as well as the daily operation of my teams. I’m successful working independently but enjoy collaborating and working with others too. I have been recognized for my consistent model work ethic. One who is proactive about improving systems, willing to collaborate and brings their whole self to work. As a software developer I’m ready for a new challenge. I enjoy problem solving, making things work and learning new skills. As a leader I demonstrate a reliable and trustworthy work ethic and expect the same for those that report to me. I’m looking for a position that will provide a flexible work life balance and one that I can consistently improve and make an impact. When I’m not at work I enjoy playing the cello and spending time with my family and friends.
